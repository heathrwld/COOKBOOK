Caesar Salad

A Caesar salad is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice, olive oil, eggs, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan and black pepper.

instructions

step 1

Whisk together minced garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.

step 2

Whisking while adding oil emulsifies the dressing for a smooth and creamy (not oily) consistency.

step 3

Season with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp black pepper.

ingredients

1 Large or 2 Small Heads of Romaine Lettuce

Parmesan cheese, Shredded or Shaved

Crisp Croutons

Caesar Salad Dressing
